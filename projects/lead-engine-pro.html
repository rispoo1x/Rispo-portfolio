What it does

Lead Engine Pro is an end-to-end automation built entirely in n8n that eliminates every manual step between a prospect filling out a form and a sales rep having a fully enriched, ready-to-close lead in their CRM.

The workflow triggers the instant a webhook fires from the lead form. It then enriches the contact with public data, applies conditional logic to score and route the lead, syncs everything to HubSpot, fires a personalised email sequence via Resend, and pings the right Slack channel — all within seconds.

Built to handle high volume without breaking. Every step has error handling, retry logic, and a fallback notification so nothing silently disappears in the middle of the night.

⚡

Webhook-triggered in real time

Smart lead scoring & routing

Conditional branches score each lead by source, company size, and intent signals — routing to the right rep automatically.

📧

Personalised email sequences

Resend fires a dynamic multi-step sequence tailored per lead score. No template blasting — real personalisation.

🔁

Full error recovery

Every node has retry logic and a fallback path. A dead API never kills the whole pipeline — it just reroutes and logs.
