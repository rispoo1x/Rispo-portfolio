What it does

Built for a creative agency that needed a landing page that would actually convert — not just look good in Figma. The brief was: fast, memorable, and built entirely without frameworks or libraries.

Every animation on the page is driven by CSS transitions and the Intersection Observer API. Entrance sequences, scroll-triggered reveals, hover effects — all native. The result is a page that loads in under a second and runs at 60fps on any device.

The copy and layout were designed around a single conversion goal: get visitors to book a discovery call. Every section, every heading, every button was written and positioned with that in mind. The call-to-action appears in the viewport within 2 seconds of landing.

🏎️

98 Lighthouse performance score

No JavaScript frameworks, no render-blocking resources, no layout shifts. The page is as fast as a page can get.

✨

Zero animation libraries

Every motion effect is CSS + Intersection Observer. No GSAP bundle, no Framer Motion — nothing to download, nothing to parse.

🎯

Conversion-first layout

Structure, copy and visual hierarchy were all designed around a single goal: booking a call. Every element earns its place.

🌍

Deployed to the edge

Hosted on Vercel with global CDN distribution. Sub-100ms TTFB from anywhere in the world thanks to edge functions.
