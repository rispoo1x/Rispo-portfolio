What it does

Invoice Autopilot is a Make workflow that handles the entire post-sale billing lifecycle. When a client signs a contract (via DocuSign or PandaDoc), the workflow triggers automatically and takes it from there.

It generates a branded PDF invoice, creates a Stripe payment link, sends the invoice to the client by email, and creates a tracked record in Notion for the team. If payment is late, it sends automated follow-up reminders on a schedule. When payment lands, it confirms receipt to the client, updates the Notion tracker, and logs the transaction.

The whole flow touches seven different services and replaces what used to be 45+ minutes of manual admin per invoice — turning it into zero minutes.

📝

Contract → invoice in one trigger

A signed contract is the only input. Everything downstream — PDF generation, Stripe links, email — fires automatically.

💳

Stripe payment tracking

Stripe webhooks feed payment status back into the workflow. Overdue? Automated reminder. Paid? Instant confirmation.

📒

Notion as the source of truth

Every invoice, status update, and payment is logged to a structured Notion database — always up to date, always queryable.

🔔

Smart reminders, not spam

Reminder cadence adapts to due date proximity. 7 days out: friendly nudge. 1 day out: urgent. Overdue: firm follow-up.
